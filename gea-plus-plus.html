Description of the project
GEA++ is a Smart Space application for children with Neurodevelopmental Disorders.

GEA++ focuses on nutrition education. The three activities proposed are based on real laboratory activities that children do in a local specialized center.

The image on the right shows the configuration of the kid and the two projectors in the smart room.
The images above display the frontal and the floor projections of the three games inside GEA++.

The interaction for the first two games is based on hand tracking while the third game relies on the pyramid smart object.
